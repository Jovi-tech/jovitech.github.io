About me

My passion for programming came about after I graduated with my BS in Electrical Engineering and got my licensed as Electrical Engineer. I was introduced to programming as a hobby, starting off with HTML/CSS and JavaScript. My first project was a portfolio website I was using to provide visualizations about my habits and some stuff that I like during my studies. That portfolio took about 3 weeks to finish, and reflecting back now, it was pretty bad. I had no idea what media queries were, it wasn't responsive, the images were pixelated, I didn't even know how to make some text go to the middle of the page, but the process made me realize that I loved creating software...
